Data protection

This website is configured to be privacy-first: no analytics, no marketing trackers, and no third-party map services are loaded.

Cookies

We do not use analytics or advertising cookies.

Tracking and analytics

This website does not use Google Analytics or any comparable visitor tracking service.

Maps and third-party services

Interactive map providers (including Mapbox, OpenStreetMap tile endpoints, and Google Maps embeds) are not integrated anymore.

No cookie consent popup is used, because optional tracking categories are not active on this site.

Please note that opening external links from this website may transfer data to the respective third-party provider.
